Best Chocolate Chips Cookies

Descripcion

“Everyone needs a good chocolate chip cookie recipe,” says culinary producer Nicole McLaughlin (a.k.a. NicoleMcMom).

"This has been my go-to chocolate chip cookie recipe since I found it on Allrecipes several years ago," says GCKJA. "The recipe is not the

'back of the bag' recipe, with different ratios of all ingredients and that's what makes these cookies so darned good!"

This chocolate chip cookie recipe makes delicious cookies with crisp edges and chewy middles

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

    Step 1
    Gather your ingredients, making sure your butter is softened, and your eggs are room temperature.
    Step 2
    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    Step 3
    Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
    Step 4
    Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
    Step 5
    Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
    Step 6
    Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
    Step 7
    Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
    Step 8
    Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.
    Step 9
    Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
    Step 10
    Store in an airtight container or serve immediately and enjoy!
